Ingredients:
 - 2 organic eggs
 - 1/4 cup of shredded cheese
 - non stick spray

Directions:
 1. Coat a frying pan with the non stick spray
 2. Heat frying pan and add two eggs
 3. Break the egg yokes
 4. After two minutes of cooking add the cheese on top of the eggs. 
 5. turn eggs over to continue to cook.
 6. Remove eggs from frying pan when cooked to desire consistency.
